Ulan-Ulan Falls Travel Guide

  • Food and water
  • Activities gear (swimwear, trekking shoes, etc.)
  • Camera for photo ops
  • Swimwear for swimming and canyoning
  • Proper footwear for trekking
  • Bravery for cliff jumping üòÑ

Travel to Barangay Sampao (20-30 mins).

  1. Rent a habal-habal or bring your own vehicle.
  2. Get off at the signage leading to the waterfalls.
  3. Hire a local guide or follow the trail.

Via Your Own Vehicle
  • Round trip fuel cost: ~Php 80 (2 liters)
  • Guide tip: Php 100 (optional)
Via Habal-Habal
  • One-way trip: Php 80 minimum
  • Round trip (pakyaw): Php 300
  • Guide tip: Php 100 (optional)
Via Rent-A-Motorcycle
  • Daily rental: Php 300 minimum + gas
  • With driver: negotiable and may include a free guide

  • Bring enough food and water
  • No mobile signal in the area
  • Charge phones/cameras beforehand
  • Do not travel alone
  • Leave before sunset
  • Wear quality trainers or sandals
  • Avoid visiting during rainy weather
